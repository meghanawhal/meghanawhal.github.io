Megha Nawhal

I am a Research Software Engineer at Google Research.

Earlier, I finished my PhD from the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University advised by Greg Mori. My PhD dissertation focused on designing novel compositional models for human activity videos. During my PhD, I also did research internships at Facebook Reality Labs, Google Research, Adobe Research and Borealis AI (Vancouver).

Previously, I worked as a Research Scientist in the Cognitive Industry Solutions group at IBM Research, Bangalore. Earlier, I graduated from IIT Kanpur where I completed my Master's thesis under the supervision of K S Venkatesh on improved scene capture methods in unfavorable lighting conditions. I also spent time at Sony Corporation, Japan as an intern in Summer 2013.
